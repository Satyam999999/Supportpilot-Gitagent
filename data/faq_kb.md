Q: How do I reset my account password?
A: Go to login, select Forgot Password, and follow the email link. If you do not receive an email in 5 minutes, check spam and then contact support.

Q: My payment failed but the money was deducted. What should I do?
A: Temporary authorization holds can appear as deductions. Wait up to 24 hours. If unresolved, share transaction ID with billing support.

Q: How do I upgrade from free plan to pro plan?
A: Open Settings > Billing > Plans, choose Pro, and complete checkout.

Q: Where can I download invoice receipts?
A: Navigate to Settings > Billing > Invoices and click Download on the desired invoice.

Q: What is your refund policy for annual plans?
A: Annual plans are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase unless prohibited by local law.

Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication?
A: Go to Settings > Security, enable 2FA, scan the QR code with an authenticator app, and confirm with OTP.

Q: How can I contact live support?
A: Use in-app chat from Help Center between 9 AM and 6 PM IST, Monday to Friday.

Q: Why am I seeing API rate limit errors?
A: Rate limit errors occur when request volume exceeds your plan limits. Retry with exponential backoff or upgrade limits.

Q: How long does support take to respond?
A: Standard support response time is within 24 business hours, while priority tickets are usually handled within 4 business hours.

Q: How do I change my registered email address?
A: Go to Settings > Account > Email, verify your current credentials, and confirm the new email through the verification link.

Q: Can I transfer my subscription to another account?
A: Subscription transfer is available only after account ownership verification by support. Submit a request from Help Center > Billing.

Q: What is included in the enterprise plan?
A: The enterprise plan includes dedicated support, advanced security controls, custom SLAs, and higher API throughput limits.

Q: Is there a student discount?
A: Student discounts are available in select regions with valid student verification. Check the pricing page or contact billing support.

Q: How do I report a bug in the dashboard?
A: Submit a report from Help Center > Report Bug with steps, expected behavior, and screenshots.

Q: How can I cancel auto-renewal?
A: Go to Settings > Billing > Subscription and toggle off auto-renewal before the next billing date.
